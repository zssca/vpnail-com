Research the following competitors and compile their pricing/promotions:

**Direct Competitors (Victoria Park):**
- Search for nail salons within 5km radius of Victoria Park, Calgary
- Focus on: Downtown Victoria Park Area

**Key Research Questions:**
1. What are their prices for equivalent services?
2. What promotions/specials are they currently running?
3. What day-specific deals do they offer (e.g., "Manic Monday", "Wellness Wednesday")?
4. Do they have loyalty/rewards programs?
5. What makes them stand out on Google reviews?
6. What are their Google ratings and review volume?
